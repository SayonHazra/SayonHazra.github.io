About This Project

This fabrication toolkit was developed by Sayon Hazra as a comprehensive solution for modern engineering workflows. The primary objective of this application is to streamline complex metal fabrication calculations, minimize human error, and enhance shop-floor efficiency.

By digitizing material weight estimations and unit conversions, this tool enables engineers, fabricators, and project managers to generate precise Bill of Materials (BOM) rapidly. It is built to bridge the gap between technical design and practical execution, ensuring that accurate data is accessible at any stage of the production lifecycle.
